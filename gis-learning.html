About This Journey

My GIS journey began with curiosity about how maps and location intelligence solve real-world problems. Through Esri training, practical exercises and personal projects, I have developed skills in Web GIS, ArcGIS Online, Survey123, Dashboards, StoryMaps, Instant Apps and Experience Builder. Every screenshot on this page represents a milestone in my growth.
